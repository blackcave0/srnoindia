ORGANIZATIONAL STRENGTH

SRNO India has successfully executed many assignments which include socio-economic surveys, monitoring &evaluation studies, capacity building & training programmes, etc. Besides providing implementation support to various Government and Non-Government Organizations, the organization has also developed core competencies in the areas of Research and Consultancy.

COLLABORATION & COORDINATION

The organization consists of qualified and experience academicians, researchers and Field Experts who ensure quality of data collected in every project. The experts not only oversee the field operations but also equip the field staff with relevant knowledge, tools, information to ensure quality of data collected for each and every project. The organization believes “Garbage in, garbage out”. To overcome this, strict quality controls at every step of the project during data collection ensures quality of data collected.

QUALITY OF WORK

SRNO India is recognized for providing quality data as per client’s specifications. The organization integrates qualitative and quantitative techniques to come up with more evidence based results. Our growing list of satisfied clients stand testimony to this fact.  

EXTENSIVE OUTREACH

SRNO India is one of the leading survey, research & analysis, capacity building firms in the country. 

The organization has worked in more than 18 states and in more than 1000 villages across the length and breadth of the country. 

The organization has successfully executed more than 215 projects for various International NGOs, Government departments, etc.

OUR UNIQUE SERVICE PROPOSITIONS

We use ODK/Survey CTO, the best way to gather data on a mobile method. Our clients select us because of our years of expertise delivering well-designed data collection and managing large data collection campaigns all over the nation.

Technologies

SRNO is using ODK, SurveyCTO, and Quest (Android-based applications) as data collection tools.

Unbiased Response

We use methods so that only the respondent can see and hear the questions and other details, and we can get an anonymous impartial response.
